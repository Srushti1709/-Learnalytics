📘 Model Evaluation Concepts

Mean Squared Error

Mean Squared Error calculates the average squared difference between actual values and predicted values. Lower values indicate better prediction performance.

Mean Absolute Error

Mean Absolute Error measures the average magnitude of prediction errors. It tells how far predictions are from actual values.

R² Score

R² score indicates how well the model explains variance in the dataset. A score closer to 1 means the model fits the data very well.

Classification Accuracy

Accuracy represents the percentage of correct predictions made by a classification model.
