📘 Understanding Performance Metrics

Accuracy

Accuracy represents the percentage of correct predictions made by the model. Higher accuracy means the model predicts the correct output most of the time.

Precision

Precision measures how many predicted positive cases were actually correct. It helps evaluate false positive errors.

Recall

Recall indicates how well the model identifies actual positive cases. It measures the ability to capture relevant instances.

F1 Score

F1 score is the harmonic mean of precision and recall. It balances both metrics to evaluate the overall performance.
