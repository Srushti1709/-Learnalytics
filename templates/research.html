📊 Research & Insights

This section presents research insights derived from the dataset used in this project. The analysis focuses on understanding how different factors influence student academic performance.

🎯 Research Objective

📚 Study Behaviour

Understand how study hours impact exam performance and whether consistent study patterns improve academic outcomes.

🏫 Attendance Impact

Analyze how attendance percentage affects the overall score and identify whether regular class participation improves results.

🔥 Motivation Factor

Evaluate whether student motivation level influences exam scores and study patterns.

📈 Performance Prediction

Use machine learning models to predict academic performance based on study behaviour and historical scores.

🧠 Research Methodology

1️⃣ Data Collection

The dataset contains multiple attributes including hours studied, attendance percentage, previous scores and motivation levels.

2️⃣ Data Preprocessing

Cleaning missing values, converting categorical variables and preparing the dataset for machine learning algorithms.

3️⃣ Model Building

Different regression and classification models were built to analyze and predict student performance.

4️⃣ Evaluation

Models were evaluated using performance metrics such as accuracy, MSE, MAE and R² score.

📊 Key Research Findings

📈 Study Hours

Students studying more than 6 hours per day showed significantly better performance compared to those studying less than 3 hours.

🎯 Attendance

Attendance above 80% strongly correlates with higher exam scores and better academic consistency.

🔥 Motivation

Highly motivated students demonstrate stronger study habits and better exam performance.

🚀 Future Research Scope

🤖 Advanced AI Models

Future work can explore deep learning models to improve prediction accuracy.

📊 Larger Dataset

Using larger and more diverse datasets can help produce more reliable predictions.

📱 Real-time Learning Analytics

Integration with online learning platforms could provide real-time student performance insights.
